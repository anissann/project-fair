Reservations

The Lab menu is available upon reservation, and intended for the whole table.

Please note that it is not possible to reserve a table outside. Each reservation will be assigned to a table inside the restaurant.

You can cancel or modify your reservation (subject to availability) free of charge up to 48 hours before the booked time. If you cancel or modify your reservation after the expiry of 48 hours or in case of no-show, the restaurant reserves the right to debit the amount of â‚¬150 on your card.

For bookings more than 4 seats please contact us directly on the number: 071 65463 (from 10:00-11:30 and 17:00-19:00) or contact us by email at prenotazioniuliassi@gmail.com.
